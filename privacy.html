Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 March 2026
Bodhanika is a free educational platform for students in Classes 1–10. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and how we use it.

1. Who We Are

Bodhanika ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website bodhanika.in — a free, hands-on learning platform offering interactive science, maths, EVS, and life skills experiments for students in Classes 1–10.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: hello@bodhanika.in

2. Information We Collect

Bodhanika does not require users to create an account or provide any personal information to use the platform. We collect the following information automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, experiments viewed, time spent, device type, browser type
  • Technical data — IP address (anonymised), approximate geographic location (city/country level), referring website
  • Preference data — your chosen theme (dark/light) stored locally on your device only

We do not collect: names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or any personally identifiable information from users or children.

3. How We Collect Information

We use the following third-party services to collect analytics data:

Google Analytics 4
We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use Bodhanika. Google Analytics collects anonymised usage data. IP addresses are anonymised before storage. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Microsoft Clarity
We use Microsoft Clarity to analyse user behaviour through heatmaps and session recordings. Clarity does not collect personally identifiable information. All recordings are anonymised.
Microsoft's Privacy Policy: privacy.microsoft.com

Google AdSense
We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other sites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
Google AdSense Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Cookies

Bodhanika uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — storing your theme preference (dark/light mode) locally on your device. These never leave your device.
  • Analytics cookies — set by Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand usage patterns.
  • Advertising cookies — set by Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how the site displays advertisements but will not affect access to educational content.

5. Children's Privacy

Bodhanika is designed for use by children aged 6–16, typically with parental or teacher guidance. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • We do not require registration or account creation
  • We do not display behavioural advertising targeted at children
  • Parents or guardians may contact us at hello@bodhanika.in with any concerns

6. How We Use Information

The anonymised usage data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand which experiments are most popular and improve content
  • Identify technical issues and fix them
  • Understand our audience to make better educational decisions
  • Display relevant advertisements to support the free service

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties beyond the analytics and advertising services listed above.

7. Third-Party Links

Bodhanika may contain links to external resources (such as reference materials). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Data Security

Bodhanika is a static website — we do not operate servers that store user data. All data collected by Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Google AdSense is stored on their respective secure infrastructure under their privacy policies.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data including:

  • Right to know what data is collected about you
  • Right to opt out of personalised advertising
  • Right to request deletion of your data (contact the respective third-party services directly)

For Indian users, these rights are governed by the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Bodhanika after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
